Career and Technical Education (CTE) Programs

At Information Technoloy High School, our Career and Technical Education (CTE) programs are designed to equip students with practical skills, knowledge, and experience needed to succeed in today’s rapidly evolving job market. These hands-on programs offer students the opportunity to explore a wide range of career pathways while earning industry certifications, gaining real-world experience, and preparing for both college and future careers.

Health Sciences

Explore careers in healthcare, including medical assisting, nursing, and emergency medical services (EMS).

Information Technology

Gain expertise in computer programming, network administration, and cybersecurity to excel in the digital workforce.

Business and Marketing

Learn about entrepreneurship, marketing strategies, accounting, and business management for future success.

Engineering and Manufacturing

Develop skills in design, robotics, and advanced manufacturing technologies for careers in engineering and construction.

Hospitality and Culinary Arts

Learn the art of cooking, hospitality management, and event planning for careers in the food service and tourism industries.

Agricultural Science

Gain hands-on experience in farming, animal care, and sustainable agricultural practices for careers in the environmental sciences.

Through rigorous coursework, internships, and industry partnerships, our CTE programs offer students not just academic knowledge, but the essential skills that employers value most—problem-solving, teamwork, communication, and technical proficiency.

Whether students are looking to enter the workforce directly after graduation or pursue higher education in their chosen field, our CTE programs provide a solid foundation for success. At Information Technoloy High School, we are committed to helping students achieve their career goals and build the future they envision.
